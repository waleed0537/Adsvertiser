Adsvertiser team FAQ

General
Information about the platform
Is it free to open a Adsvertiser account?
Yes, it's completely free to open a Adsvertiser account. You can sign up without any registration fees or setup costs. You only pay when you run advertising campaigns or earn revenue as a publisher.
What do you need from me to start?
To get started, you'll need to provide basic registration information including your email address, contact details, and payment information. For publishers, you'll also need to submit your website for approval.
How You Can Contact Us
You can contact us through multiple channels: live chat support on our platform, email at support@adsvertiser.com, or through our ticket system in your account dashboard. We also have dedicated account managers for high-volume clients.
How to close my account?
To close your account, contact our support team through the ticket system or email. Make sure to withdraw any remaining balance and stop all active campaigns before requesting account closure.
Can I work as an Advertiser and Publisher using the same account?
Yes, you can operate as both an advertiser and publisher using the same Adsvertiser account. This allows you to monetize your traffic while also purchasing advertising for your campaigns from one unified dashboard.
Advertising
Tips and guidelines for advertisers
How to set up tracking conversions?
Set up conversion tracking by implementing the Adsvertiser tracking pixel on your conversion page, or use postback URLs to send conversion data. You can also integrate with third-party tracking platforms like Voluum or RedTrack.
Retargeting
Retargeting allows you to show ads to users who previously visited your website. Create retargeting audiences by implementing our tracking pixel, then create campaigns targeting these specific user segments for better conversion rates.
Optimizer
Our Optimizer is an AI-powered tool that automatically optimizes your campaigns for better performance. It analyzes traffic sources, creatives, and targeting to improve your ROI by pausing underperforming elements and scaling winning combinations.
What is CPA?
CPA (Cost Per Action) is a pricing model where you pay only when a user completes a specific action like making a purchase, signing up, or downloading an app. This model helps ensure you only pay for actual results.
How can I create a campaign?
To create a campaign, log into your account and click "Create Campaign". Choose your ad format, set targeting parameters, upload creatives, define your budget and bidding strategy, then submit for approval. Most campaigns are approved within 24 hours.
Publishing
Tips and guidelines for Publishers
How do I add a website and ad spots?
To add a website, go to your Publisher dashboard and click "Add Website". Enter your domain, select content categories, and submit for approval. Once approved, create ad spots by specifying dimensions, placement locations, and ad formats.
Payment Methods and Terms for Publishers
Publishers are paid NET15 (15 days after month end). Payment methods include wire transfer, PayPal, Payoneer, and cryptocurrency. Minimum payout is $100. Payments are processed automatically once you reach the threshold.
How can I get started as a Publisher?
To start as a publisher: 1) Register an account, 2) Add your website for approval, 3) Create ad spots with appropriate sizes and placements, 4) Implement the ad codes on your site, 5) Monitor performance and optimize placements for better revenue.
Google's Misleading Ad Restrictions
Google has strict policies against misleading ads. Ensure your ads are honest, accurate, and don't use deceptive tactics like fake news formats, misleading claims, or clickbait. Adsvertiser also enforces quality standards to maintain user trust.
Developers Knowledgebase
Our developers knowledgebase contains technical documentation, API references, integration guides, and code examples. It includes information on implementing tracking pixels, setting up postbacks, and integrating with our API for automated campaign management.
API
For developers to interact with our system
 
How to fetch stats for advertiser campaigns
Use the /reports/campaigns endpoint with your API token to fetch campaign statistics. You can filter by date range, campaign ID, and specify metrics like impressions, clicks, conversions, and costs. Data is updated in real-time.
How to create an access token
Go to your Profile page, navigate to the API section, and click "Generate API Key". You can then use this key with our authentication endpoint to generate access tokens for API calls. Tokens expire after 24 hours for security.
How to create advertiser campaign via API
Use the POST /campaigns endpoint with required parameters including name, ad format, targeting settings, budget, bid, and creative assets. The campaign will be submitted for moderation and you'll receive a campaign ID upon successful creation.
